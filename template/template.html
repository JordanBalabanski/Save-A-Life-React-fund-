Welcome to "Save a life"!

The idea of "Save a life" is to help animals in need. Here you can either make a posts for an animal you've found and help it find a new home, or be the one giving it shelter. Every post has photos, title and description with information about the animal, plus contact information. Also there is a comment section if you can't seem to contact the user. Everything is free in the name of the animals.

From all of us in "Save a life", we thank you for joining us and helping our team to make this world a better place!
